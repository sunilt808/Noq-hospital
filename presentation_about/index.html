REVIEWED BY HEGEMII

NOQ

Smart Hospital Queue & Appointment System

An innovative digital healthcare platform transforming chaotic outpatient departments into seamless, real-time experiences.

Developed by Sunil T

Abstract & Introduction

Traditional hospitals rely on manual paper registers. This leads to physical crowding, lost records, and zero transparency for patients.

NOQ introduces a full-stack digital solution. It replaces the physical waiting room with a live digital token system, bridging the gap between administrators, doctors, and patients on a unified platform.

The Core Idea

To give patients back their time while ensuring hospital efficiency. Patients track their live token remotely and arrive exactly when the doctor is ready, eliminating waiting room chaos.

What makes NOQ Different?

Absolute Equality

No VIP treatment. The system strictly enforces queue order, ensuring fair and equal treatment for all patients.

Geofenced Booking

To prevent spam, regular tokens can only be booked when the patient is in the physical surroundings of the hospital.

Zero-Touch Revenue

Immediate 15/80/5 percentage split (Doc/HM/Admin) logged exactly when a consultation completes.

The Tech Stack

Built for scale, speed, and asynchronous processing.

  • Frontend: React 19, Vite, Tailwind CSS
  • Backend: Python FastAPI, Uvicorn, Pydantic
  • Database: MongoDB (Motor Async)
  • Security: JWT, PBKDF2 Hashing

Why MongoDB & FastAPI?

Migrated from Firebase to eliminate NoSQL read quota limits during heavy dashboard analytical queries. FastAPI provides asynchronous request handling, making the backend incredibly fast.

System Architecture

Client Layer (React)

Role-Based Single Page Application.

API Layer (FastAPI)

REST Endpoints & JWT Security.

Data Layer (MongoDB)

Dynamic Document Storage.

ROLE 1 / 4

The Admin

The global overseer of the NOQ platform. Ensures compliance, approves new hospitals to prevent fraud, and monitors platform health.

Admin: Detailed Functions

Hospital Approvals

Reviews registration requests from Hospital Managers. Changes status from 'Pending' to 'Active'.

Platform Revenue

Tracks the 5% platform commission aggregated across all hospitals in the system.

Audit & Compliance

Full access to global Audit Logs tracking IP addresses for every CREATE, UPDATE, or DELETE action.

ROLE 2 / 4

Hospital Manager

The local administrator. Responsible for managing the daily operations of a specific hospital, including doctors, rooms, and queues.

Manager: Detailed Functions

Staffing & Rooms

Performs CRUD operations for Doctors, Departments, and assigns consulting rooms.

Queue Monitoring

Monitors active queues. Can pause or reset queues in case of doctor emergencies.

Complaint Action

Reviews patient complaints. Can issue warnings or suspend doctors from the platform.

ROLE 3 / 4

The Doctor

The core medical provider. Uses a highly streamlined dashboard to handle patient flow rapidly without administrative distractions.

Doctor: Detailed Functions

Call Next

Moves the queue forward. Changes token status from 'Waiting' to 'Calling'. Tracks absentees.

Digital Prescriptions

Writes prescriptions tracking dosage, duration, and refill counts directly in the DB.

Patient History

Instantly views the patient's past medical records and previous prescriptions before consultation.

ROLE 4 / 4

The Patient

The end-user. Gains total control over their healthcare journey, escaping physical waiting rooms for a fully digital experience with strict accountability.

Patient: Detailed Functions

Geofenced Booking

Regular tokens must be booked from the hospital surroundings to prevent spam and ensure genuine attendance.

Accountability

Patients can report doctors. Conversely, continuous patient absence leads to system warnings and minor penalty fines.

Billing & Records

Accesses consultation bills and downloads PDF versions of their digital prescriptions.

BUSINESS RULES

Advanced Features

NOQ enforces strict logic to maintain order and fairness across the hospital network.

  • At-Home Advanced Booking: Strictly limited to Pregnancy cases, Babies (<7y), and Elders (70+). All others must book locally.
  • No Emergency Cases: App strictly handles OPD. Emergencies bypass the app entirely for direct physical care.
  • True Equality: No VIP fast-tracking. The system forces sequential token fulfillment.

Technical Highlights

  • JWT Bearer Security: Stateless 24-hour sessions.
  • Centralized Audit Logging: HIPAA-concept compliant IP tracking.
  • Soft Deletion: Data is never hard-deleted to preserve integrity.
  • Atomic MongoDB Operations: Prevents booking race conditions.
  • Pydantic Validation: Bulletproof API payload parsing.
  • Custom Axios Hooks: Auto-retry on 500 errors.

Upcoming Features

  • AI Wait Time: ML models predicting delays.
  • Telemedicine: Video consultations via WebRTC.
  • WhatsApp Alerts: Twilio API integration.

Limitations (Cons)

  • Internet Dependency: Requires active connectivity.
  • Smartphone Required: Relies on patients having devices.
  • NoSQL Integrity: Lacks strict foreign-key constraints.

Conclusion

NOQ successfully transforms traditional, paper-based queuing into a transparent digital experience with strict accountability protocols. By migrating to a robust FastAPI + MongoDB architecture, it provides a highly scalable foundation capable of powering modern hospital networks equitably.
